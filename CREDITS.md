## How to Contribute

Campaign Stack is built on a foundation of open-source generosity. You can give back by:

1. **Contributing improvements** - See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)
2. **Reporting bugs** - Help us fix issues
3. **Sharing feedback** - Let us know what works and what doesn't

5. **Sharing knowledge** - Document what you learn and help others
